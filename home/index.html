Pan African Women Economic Summit 2022

Building a transformative economy in the entrepreneurial ecosystem for Africa

Get Started

ABOUT

Tanzania Women CEOs Roundtable (TWCR) in Collaboration with National Economic Empowerment Council (NEEC) jointly host “The Pan-African Women Economic Summit 2022” on the 26-28th July 2022, Serena hotel, Dar es Salaam, Tanzania.

Overview

WHY ATTEND


Enhance your leadership.

Interactive session with prominent leaders starting with a master class that is designed to give you the skills to harness your leadership potentials

Build and Connect.

A network of women leaders in the continent for cross border trade, enhancing the AFCTFTA agreement, investments with strength in collaboration, sharing and engaging from mutual trust.

Be Inspired.

CEOs, Top Executives, funders, policy makers and women leaders and business led owners, experts, sharing knowledge, skills and opportunities in sectorial Roundtables.

Wellness

This summit has been designed to give high priority to the well-being by providIng in the program casual social networking , entertainment and Tours



Overview

WHO TO ATTEND


A very diverse Audience.

The summit is expected to bring together youth, leading African women, entrepreneurs, CEOs, members of both public/private sector, the diplomatic corps, policy makers, UN Agencies, and Senior Government officials. Key delegates titles include:
Founders | CEOs | COOs | CFOs | CHROs | Managers | Directors | C-Suite Executives

  • Objectives

  • Key Topics

Program:

    An interactive session for all participants on the Key Topics.

  • To empower women with skills through targeted master classes to create a sustainable entrepreneurial eco-system.
  • To access productive and financial resources for women entrepreneurs; 
  • To Amplify Women’s voice and visibility in leadership position.
  • To establish strategic partnerships for Cross border trade under the AFCFTA & Exhibit Products made in Africa and beyond.
  • Financing valuable projects through pitching in Manufacturing, agriculture, and ICT.
  • Preparing and engaging Youth as future leaders and entrepreneurs.
  • To recognize, appreciate and celebrate youth, men and women who are:
    • Change agents
    • Trendsetters
    • Impactful in business women and leadership

Key Topics:

  • Digital Financial Inclusion & Access to finance to productive resources.
  • Green economy for the sustainability of African environment.
  • Innovations and Technology in transformation in business.
  • Industrialisation and Infrastructure Development.
  • Building resilient economy in post-Covid19 (Health & Tourism).
  • Roundtables of Associations and Networks collaborating in support to the 4th Industrial Revolution.
  • Opportunities in mining and Extractive Industries (oil & gas) for youth and women.
  • Blue economy- embracing Women in Maritime, fishing and sea products.
