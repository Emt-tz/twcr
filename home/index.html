ABOUT TWCR

ABOUT

Tanzania Women CEOs Roundtable Limited aims at building the capacity of individual women business leaders and entities including Women organisations, NGOs, CBOs, FBOs that focuses on the objectives of the company through among other means; coaching, training and mentoring. Our events showcase Women CEOs together with men championing in partnership with other key stakeholders in Business for future growth in the economy of the country hence continent.

Our objectives:

  • Promote women excellence in leadership.
  • Advocate women economic empowerment through leadership.
  • Improve women leaders’ social stature and accelerate progressive interests.
  • Promote unity and peer support among business women leader
Mission

To provide a platform of Executive leaders for Networking and peer level. (Business executives).

To Value the accomplishment, challenge your prospective and support the drive to success.




Vision

Is an environment of mutual trust, integrity, and caring, where members are truthful and honest with each other, offering and receiving counsel, helping each member generate and maintain accountability to self, and pointing out blind spots.
